About Me: Your Drug Discovery Data Scientist

Hello! I'm a passionate and driven data scientist specializing in the fascinating field of drug discovery. My journey involves navigating vast datasets and scientific literature to uncover insights that accelerate the identification and development of novel therapeutics.

My expertise is at the intersection of biology, chemistry, and data science, focusing on transforming complex biological and chemical information into actionable data for research and development.

My Core Expertise Includes:

  • Bioinformatics: Analyzing biological data, including genomics, proteomics, and transcriptomics, to understand disease mechanisms and identify potential drug targets.
  • Cheminformatics: Utilizing computational methods to store, manage, and analyze chemical information, particularly related to small molecules and their interactions with biological systems.
  • Data Mining & Curation: Extracting relevant information from public databases like NCBI, RCSB PDB, PubChem, ChEMBL, and other scientific literature repositories.
  • Computational Drug Design: Applying computational techniques such as molecular docking, virtual screening, and QSAR modeling to predict and optimize drug-like properties.
  • Scientific Communication: Translating complex scientific findings into clear and concise information, making it accessible to diverse audiences.

This "Drug Discovery Data Scientist's Toolkit" is my contribution to the community, aiming to provide a readily accessible compilation of essential online resources for fellow researchers and enthusiasts in the drug discovery space.
